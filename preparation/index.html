All forms of life should be treated equally and not born to be exploited as mere food. Human interference in the natural order has caused multiple impacts on other living beings. Human civilization is playing the role of a dictator on Earth, being both selfish and arrogant. The game developers aim to convey the interconnectedness and commonality of life through this game, emphasizing that all life forms deserve respect and urging people to value the interdependence and mutual respect among different forms of life. It serves as a reminder to cherish the rights of all living beings and don't be a troublemaker in the ecosystem!

With our work, let us unite in understanding the value of every life and work towards
a harmonious coexistence with all living beings on our planet.

　　At Candy Entertainment Independent Game Studio, we are a passionate group of individuals who share a love for gaming. We firmly believe in the boundless power of games to inspire and bring people from all walks of life together. Our mission is to promote interconnectedness and harmony among all living beings, while advocating for equal respect for the value of each creature. Through our games, we aim to challenge traditional gaming norms and reflect our core values.

　　With a team of finest creative experts, we continuously push the boundaries of innovation, injecting fresh inspiration into the gaming industry. Our unparalleled game development capabilities enable us to create immersive gaming experiences that captivate players and ignite their imaginations. We strive for excellence in every aspect of our creations, from intricate gameplay mechanics to compelling storytelling.

　　In addition to our exceptional game development prowess, Our talented music production team excels at composing awe-inspiring and emotionally charged music that enhances the overall gaming experience. We firmly believe that a powerful and evocative score can transport players to new realms and deepen their connection with the game world.

　　As a studio, we pride ourselves on our brand expansion abilities. Our core focus revolves around building strong and recognizable game IPs that resonate with players. With ownership of the intellectual property rights to our core characters, we can nurture and evolve these beloved figures, creating a profound connection with players. Furthermore, we have the independence to publish and market our games, allowing us to express our unique creative vision without compromise.

　　Driven by passion, we aim to create impactful works that leave a lasting impression on players. Through our games, we not only entertain but also share the beauty of our values with players around the world. We believe that by sharing our passion and embracing diverse experiences, we can foster harmonious coexistence between humans and all living beings, inspiring positive change.

　　Join us on this extraordinary journey as we unleash the full potential of gaming, creating works that resonate with hearts and minds worldwide. Let's together explore new horizons and shape a world that cherishes and celebrates the beauty of every life.
